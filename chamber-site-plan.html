Abeokuta Chamber of Commerce

Site-Plan

Overview

Purpose

The Abeokuta Chamber of Commerce is your partner in business. The chamber's sole mission is to nurture an environment that recognizes and supports the growth of small community businesses in the Abeokuta area. This chamber was chartered in 2023, at Ibara Local Government Area. The Abeokuta Chamber yearly calendar is packed full of events hosted by the chambers. These events are not limited to Craft fairs and monthly networking, but there is a variety such as Young Professional Group, workshops, seminars, and multi-chamber events.

Audience

Our audience is a potential business person who is craving the growth of their personal business and the community at large. Start-ups, Entrepreneurs, community investors who believe investing is the right to do in rowing the community, professional business personals, business mentors and gurus, and those who are more interested in the growth of their $ ROI, with the interest of the community as well.

Scenarios

  1. There are different events on your website, but how can I get involved?
  2. What is the contact information for the chamber?
  3. Do you host mentorship program and how frequent do you host such events?
