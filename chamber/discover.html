Abeokuta Demography

  • Abeokuta is the capital city of Ogun State in southwest Nigeria. It is situated on the east bank of the Ogun River, near a group of rocky outcrops in a wooded savanna; 77 kilometres north of Lagos by railway, or 130 kilometres by water.
  • ❄️ State: Ogun State
  • ❄️ County: Abeokuta South
  • ❄️ Population: 571,499
  • ❄️ Altitude: 66 m (217 ft)
